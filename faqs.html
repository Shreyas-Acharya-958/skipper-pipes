SKipper Pipes

India’s Safest Pipes

At Skipper Pipes, we are committed to transparency, customer support, and industry leadership. Whether you're a homeowner, contractor, distributor, or architect — this FAQ section addresses your most common queries about our products, services, and installation practices

General Questions

Skipper Pipes offers a wide range of piping and water storage solutions including UPVC, CPVC, SWR, HDPE, Column Pipes, Casing Pipes, Ribbed Strainer Pipes, Agricultural Pipes, and Marina Water Tanks. Our products serve domestic, industrial, agricultural, and infrastructure needs.

Our products are lead-free, UV-stabilized, corrosion-resistant, and conform to national and international quality standards (ISI, ASTM, etc.). Advanced co-moulded seals, strict testing protocols, and R&D-backed innovation ensure long-lasting, leak-free performance.

Product Performance & Quality

Installation & Maintenance

While many of our products are designed for easy handling, we recommend hiring a certified plumber or contractor to ensure proper jointing, alignment, and sealing — especially for high-pressure or concealed systems.

Skipper offers standardized dimensions conforming to Indian and global norms. However, for best performance and zero leakage, we recommend using Skipper fittings with Skipper pipes.

Our pipes require minimal maintenance. Periodic inspection for joints and external impact is sufficient. In drainage systems, occasional flushing with clean water helps maintain optimal flow.
