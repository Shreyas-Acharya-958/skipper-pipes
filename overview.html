Who We Are!

Skipper Pipes is a leading Indian manufacturer of lead-free, high-performance piping systems trusted by engineers, builders, farmers, and homeowners alike. From residential plumbing to large-scale infrastructure, our products deliver unmatched safety, strength, and longevity—backed by 16+ years of R&D and a pan-India service network.
