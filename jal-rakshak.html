About the Initiative

Why #JalRakshak?

Skipper Jalrakshak - SAVE WATER, SAVE LIFE

Skipper Pipes is proud to launch a very special initiative on water conservation -Skipper Jalrakshak - SAVE WATER, SAVE LIFE.

Water is life’s purest blessing, yet today it is one of our most endangered resources. Durga Puja, a festival that celebrates strength, tradition, and togetherness, is the perfect time to unite as a community with a shared purpose - to safeguard water for our children and the generations to come.

Through Skipper Jalrakshak, we aim to inspire awareness, responsibility, and collective action. This initiative flows into the heart of Puja celebrations, reminding us that true devotion lies not only in rituals but also in protecting nature

To add to the festive spirit, the event will be hosted by the award-winning RJ Praveen and his team, bringing their humour, energy, and warmth to engage both children and parents alike.

We warmly invite you and your children to join us on (Date, Time, Venue) and be part of this meaningful celebration.
